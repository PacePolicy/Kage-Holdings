Mark Carney Campaign Promises

The Liberal Party promises to do the following:

  • Reduce internal trade costs by up to 15%
  • Expand economy by up to $200 billion (about $5,000 per Canadian)
  • Table legislation by Canada Day to eliminate all federal barriers to interprovincial trade and labour mobility
  • Achieve mutual recognition of professional credentials across provinces
  • Mandate federal departments to review and report within 60 days on regulatory streamlining
  • Prioritize major economic projects that span provincial/territorial boundaries

Mark Carney’s government will invest in large-scale infrastructure to physically and economically unite Canada, strengthen our sovereignty, and power long-term growth through clean energy and trade corridors.

  • Support projects like: Port of Churchill, Edmonton hydrogen, Cowessess solar, Windsor–Québec City high-speed rail, Grays Bay corridor, George Massey Tunnel, and Trans-Canada twinning.
  • Make the Confederation Bridge, Marine Atlantic, and other ferries more affordable, especially for PEI residents.
  • Invest in a new $5 billion Trade Diversification Corridors Fund for ports, rail, highways, and airports.
  • Double the Indigenous Loan Guarantee Program from $5B to $10B, expanding access to major project ownership.
  • Expand east-west electricity grid to connect clean power across Canada and strengthen energy security.
  • Add $250 million to the Rural Transit Solutions Fund for local and regional transit.
  • Invest in AI infrastructure, rural broadband, and reliable cell service to boost innovation and connectivity.
  • Provide disaster recovery funds to restore infrastructure affected by climate disasters.
  • Support building and upgrading health facilities, community infrastructure, and accessible housing.
