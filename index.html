Mark Carney Campaign Promises

Canada needs a unified economy to counter external threats and create growth by reducing internal trade barriers and improving labour mobility.

  • Reduce internal trade costs by up to 15%
  • Expand economy by up to $200 billion (about $5,000 per Canadian)
  • Table legislation by Canada Day to eliminate all federal barriers to interprovincial trade and labour mobility
  • Achieve mutual recognition of professional credentials across provinces
  • Mandate federal departments to review and report within 60 days on regulatory streamlining
  • Prioritize major economic projects that span provincial/territorial boundaries
